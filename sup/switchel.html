switchel

(n): a wonderful summer substitute for lemonade (or juice)

May 24, 2020

Switchel has been one of my favorite drinks for a while now. Whenever we get a good batch of local honey, my first instinct is to mix it into some cold water with lemon juice and apple cider vinegar. I would highly recommend this as a replacement for lemonade.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups of water
  • 1 tablespoon of honey
  • 1 lemon squeezed
  • 2 tablespoons of apple cider vinegar
